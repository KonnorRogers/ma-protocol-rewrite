<h2 id="response">
  Respond to scene in a safe manner:
</h2>

<ul>
<li>Review dispatch information</li>
<li>Use lights and sirens and/or pre-emptive devices when responding as appropriate per emergency medical dispatch information and local guidelines.</li>
</ul>

<br></br>

<h2 id="scene-arrival">
Scene arrival and size-up:
</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Utilize Body Substance Isolation, as appropriate.</li>
  <li>Scene safety, bystander safety.</li>
  <li>Environmental hazards assessment.</li>
  <li>Number of patients.</li>
  <li>Determine need for additional resources.</li>
  <li>Utilize Mass Casualty Incident (MCI) and/or Incident Command System (ICS) procedures as necessary.</li>
  <li>Determine mechanism of injury/illness.</li>
</ul>

<br></br>
