  </li>
  <ul>
   <li> Midazolam 2 - 6 mg slow IV/IO/IM or</li>
   <li> Midazolam 2 - 6 mg IN</li>
   <li> Magnesium sulfate 2-4 grams IV/IO over 5 minutes. </li>
  </ul>
</ul>

<EMTLevel level="paramedic" medControl={true}>
  <ul>
    <li>
      Administration of additional IV Normal Saline.
    </li>
    <li>
      Calcium chloride 10% 20 mg/kg IV/IO administer slowly over 5 minutes to a
      maximum dose of 1 gram. (Antidote for Magnesium Sulfate).
    </li>
    <li>
      Further anticonvulsant therapy.
    </li>
  </ul>
