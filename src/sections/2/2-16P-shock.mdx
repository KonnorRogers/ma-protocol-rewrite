<Note id="note-1">
  Any patient with signs, symptoms, and history suggesting inadequate
  tissue perfusion should be considered to be in shock. Make every
  effort to determine and treat the underlying cause. Regardless of
  etiology, shock patients should be transported immediately to the
  nearest appropriate facility for definitive care .
</Note>

    <li>Keep the patient supine</li>
